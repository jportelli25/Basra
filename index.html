The Classic Fishing Card Game

Basra

The classic Arabic fishing card game. Capture cards from the table by matching their value or adding them up, sweep the whole table with a Jack, and clear it in one play for a Basra. Play solo vs AI or online with friends — free.

What makes it different

🎣

Capture the table

Play a card that matches a table card's value — or one that equals the sum of several — and scoop them into your pile.

🅹

Jacks sweep

A Jack takes the entire table at once. Queens and Kings grab their own kind.

✨

Basra bonus

Clear the whole table in a single capture for a Basra — a bonus 10 points.

🤖

Solo vs AI

Three difficulty levels. Free-for-all (2–4 players) or 2v2 Partnership, with name-able opponents.

🌐

Online & cross-platform

Invite a friend, host a private room, or join a random table — live across iPhone and Android, with rematch.

🏆

Leaderboards

Global, Friends and Single-Player boards track your wins, podiums and win rate.

How it plays

  • Everyone gets 4 cards; 4 more lie face-up on the table. Tap a card to play it.
  • Capture by matching a table card's number (Ace = 1) or by playing a card equal to the sum of some table cards. A Jack sweeps everything.
  • Clear the table in one capture for a Basra (+10). The last capture takes the leftovers.
  • Score for most cards, Aces, the 10♦, the 2♣, Jacks and Basras — the best total wins the match.
